Rolex Recipe!Eggs rolled in chapati,the sweetness is of no doubt

About the Recipe

Locally made and found on the streets of Uganda, its one of the most consumed street foods in the country.

Its(rolex) enjoyed by all generations currently having its own festival held on 21st/August every year.

Tasting isnot a choice but a must

Ingredients

  • chapati
  • Eggs
  • Cabbages
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  1. Chop Cabbages,Onions and Tomatoes
  2. Crack your egg and add salt then mix
  3. Coat your frying pan with cooking oil and fry the egg for 1-2 minutes changing it both sides
  4. Put ur ready fried egg on chapati, pour the choped vegtables and roll

One bite will make you feel like another bite
